The American Red Cross

Bringing those with need much needed support.

The American Red Cross provides humanitarian aid for those in need around the world. They wish to make the world a better place by utilizing their passion and compassion to make sure that everyone can get access to the medical support, training, and supplies they need when disaster unexpectedly strikes.

The American Red Cross has three strategies to achieve its goals. First through the generosity of donors ARC can fund humanitarian aid projects in disaster areas around the world. Second through volunteers ARC has a big pool of human resources with a variety of skills to accomplish any objective they are given. Last through training people in the community the world will be better equipped to deal with disaster.

If you are feeling the itch to get involved with this grand cause there are several things you can do. You can volunteer, help from passionate people is always welcome. You can donate everything from donating blood to any amount money all will be going to a good cause. You can take some ARC training courses to become better equipped to help your community. Lastly, if you're really moved by this cause you can finish your education and apply to work for ARC.

If you want to get in touch with ARC you can use the ARC Contact Page link in the nav bar for general inquiries. Other than that, you can use the ARC homepage to find local branches of ARC to contact.
